Sol Roth

Sol Roth grew up in Eugene, Oregon as a skinny, shy kid. In high school he got his first taste of theater and was hooked. He began writing his own stories. Through fitness, dance and artistic performance, he overcame the shyness that dominated his childhood. After high-school he served in the US Army and built up his body and real life adventure experience. He attended the University of Oregon and after being inspired by the the independent filmmaking revolution he wrote, starred, and directed his first feature film. After college, Sol Roth put everything he owned in the trunk of his car and drove down to LA to pursue his dreams.

From there Sol Roth has built both a company and a body of work that spans platforms and genres. He is an athlete and fitness motivator who trains hardcore and is always pushing his mind and body, a viral dancer and performance artist with millions of views on social media, an actor in feature films, a writer of books and original movie scripts, a designer of logos and artwork, and the list goes on and on. His goal is to make works that matter and inspire others while expressing his true self... And being awesome!
